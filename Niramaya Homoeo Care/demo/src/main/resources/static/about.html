WHAT IS HOMOEOPATHY?

Homoeopathy is a system of medicine discovered by Dr. Samuel Hahnemann, it is holistic in approach and aims to cure the ailment from its root without creating side effects. Homoeopathy offers wide range of scope for various ailments, it can be followed from a new born to old aged people.

WHAT YOU NEED TO KNOW BEFORE TREATMENT?

In order to experience the best treatment, the Homoeopathy doctor needs to gather a comprehensive note of the symptoms being experienced. It would be better to explain your concerns in simple language rather than medical terms, this explanation would help the doctor choose an exact remedy there by treating the ailment in a faster manner. Homoeopathic treatment is based on the symptoms and each and every query raised by the doctor is very relevant to the treatment. In every ailment the mind is also affected, so we question in regard to emotions such as fear, anger and individual response or reaction to a particular situation, thereby fetching details regarding them will help us to serve you better.
